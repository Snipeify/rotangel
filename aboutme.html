About Me

Welcome to my world of atmospheric breakcore. I'm rotangel, a 17-year-old music producer dedicated to creating immersive soundscapes that blend chaotic rhythms with ethereal atmospheres.

My music explores the boundaries between structure and chaos, combining intricate breakbeats with ambient textures to create something uniquely atmospheric. Each track is a journey through digital landscapes and emotional depths.

I'm constantly experimenting with new sounds, textures, and rhythms, pushing the limits of what breakcore can be while maintaining its raw, energetic core.
